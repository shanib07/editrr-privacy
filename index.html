Privacy Policy for Editrr

Effective Date: June 2026

Editrr respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Editrr handles information when you use the app.

Information Processing

Editrr processes videos, audio, and lyrics primarily on your device. Your media files are not uploaded to Editrr servers.

Media Access

Editrr requests access to your Photos library so that you can import videos and save exported videos.

Lyrics Generation

Lyrics generation is performed locally on your device using downloaded AI models.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Editrr uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information.

Data Sharing

Editrr does not sell personal information and does not share your media files with third parties.
