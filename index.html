Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Introduction

Editrr respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how Editrr collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the application.

About Editrr

Editrr is an AI-powered video editing application that helps users create beat-synced short-form videos, generate lyric overlays, customize edits, preview content, and export finished videos.

Information We Collect

Editrr may access media that you choose to provide:

  • Videos from your Photos library
  • Audio files
  • Music tracks
  • Generated editing content

This media is used solely to provide editing functionality within the app.

Local Processing

Editrr is designed with a local-first architecture.

The following operations are performed primarily on your device:

  • Beat detection
  • Audio analysis
  • Timeline generation
  • Preview generation
  • Video export
  • Lyrics generation
  • Video customization

Your videos and audio files are not uploaded to Editrr-operated servers for processing.

AI Models

Some features, including lyrics generation, may require downloading AI models to your device.

  • Downloaded directly to your device
  • Stored locally
  • Reused for future generations
  • Not shared with other users

Photos Library Access

Editrr requests access to your Photos library so that you can:

  • Import videos
  • Select media for editing
  • Save exported videos

Access can be revoked at any time through iOS Settings.

Storage and Temporary Files

Editrr may temporarily create local working files during:

  • Video import
  • Audio extraction
  • Preview generation
  • Video export
  • Lyrics generation

Temporary files are automatically cleaned up when no longer required.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Editrr uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information to improve application stability and performance.

Information may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostic information

Crash reports do not intentionally contain your videos, audio files, lyrics, or exported content.

Data Sharing

Editrr does not sell personal information.

Editrr does not share your videos, audio files, or exported content with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-Party Services

Editrr may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Apple system frameworks and services

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Editrr is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Editrr relies on security mechanisms provided by Apple platforms and trusted third-party providers to help protect information.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your information, including access, correction, deletion, and restriction of processing.

Because Editrr primarily processes media locally on your device, you retain direct control over most of your information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
